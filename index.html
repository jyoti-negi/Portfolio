About

WordPress Developer / HTML Developer

With over 8 years of experience in Web Design and WordPress development, I specialize in creating clean, pixel-perfect, and fully responsive websites. I bring ideas to life by converting Figma designs, UI concepts, or rough sketches into production-ready digital experiences that are both visually appealing and technically sound.

Within WordPress, my expertise includes ACF (Advanced Custom Fields), custom theme work, plugin integrations, and component-based layouts. I also work with popular visual builders such as Divi, Elementor, and Avada, allowing for fast and flexible development without compromising quality. I’m experienced in building custom templates, child themes, dynamic content blocks, and ensuring performance, accessibility, and SEO best practices across projects.

I also develop and manage WooCommerce stores, including product configuration, variations, cart & checkout customization, email flows, and performance optimization. Beyond WordPress, I have 6 months of experience in Shopify store setup.
